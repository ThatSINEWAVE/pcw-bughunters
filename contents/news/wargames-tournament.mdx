This article is written based on the official announcements made inside the game's official Discord server.

**Announcing the Winter Wargames Tournament!**

Agents, it's time to showcase your skills! Project CW is hosting the **Winter Wargames Tournament**, allowing you to dive into competitive gameplay during the biggest-ever playtest. Assemble your squad, plan your strategies, and get ready to dominate the battlefield!

### Tournament Details
- **Game Mode:** Plant/Defuse
- **Format:** Teams of 5 players, with an optional substitute
- **Team Limit:** 64 teams

### Schedule
- **Swiss Tournament Qualifier:**
  *Saturday, January 27, 2024, 9:00 PM*
- **Single Elimination Tournament:**
  *Sunday, January 28, 2024, 9:00 PM*

For official rules, please review the [Winter Wargames Tournament Rules](https://bit.ly/WWTournament). Note that some rules and links are still being finalized and marked accordingly.

### Registration
To enter the tournament, form a team, designate a captain, and sign up using the following link: [Winter Wargames Tournament Registration](https://bit.ly/WWTRegistration).
**Registration Closes:** *Thursday, January 25, 2024, at 2:00 AM*, or when all slots are filled (first-come, first-served).

Players can use the **No Access** channel to start forming teams. For questions, reach out in the **tournament-questions** channel.
