## The Goal

Each of these images, from vibrant street signs to faded posters, contains words and symbols that, when translated, offer glimpses into the timeline, cultural influences, and setting of the game's universe. The goal of this post is to meticulously catalogue and decipher the meaning of all environmental artwork, posters, and images found in the files.

## The Images

Each image is listed with its original filename for easy reference and correlation. We’ve also organised the files into categories based on their type, making it simpler to analyse patterns and themes. Images can reveal both historical context and a glimpse into the daily lives of the characters or help determine locations of the maps within the game world.
