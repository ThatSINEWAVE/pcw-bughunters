## The Goal

Each of these images, from vibrant street signs to faded posters, contains words and symbols that, when translated, offer glimpses into the timeline, cultural influences, and setting of the game's universe. The goal of this post is to meticulously catalog and decipher the meaning of all environmental artwork, posters, and images found in the files.

## The Images

Each image is listed with its original filename for easy reference and correlation. We’ve also organized the files into categories based on their type, making it simpler to analyze patterns and themes.

### Advertisements and Billboards

Advertisements reflect the commercial side of the game’s society, often highlighting popular products, political propaganda, or societal trends. They can reveal both historical context and a glimpse into the daily lives of the characters.
